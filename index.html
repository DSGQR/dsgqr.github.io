About Me


At this juncture in my life, I have decided to take my tech knowledge to the next level. As a result I have completed an intensive 6 month coding boot camp at the University of Irvine California.

I have over a decade of experience in the sales, marketing and management industries. Prior to my work as an executive assistent and my work with BD Medical, I have held numerous positions in the customer service and sales industries, including executive assistance and numerous positions at Apple Inc.. Throughout my positions, I have had the opportunity to apply my wealth of sales talent, aptitude for customer service and track record for accomplishing projects on tight timelines.

I strive to maximize my natural given talents to constantly learn, grow and to better myself so that I may successfully reach my goals and inspire others to do the same.
